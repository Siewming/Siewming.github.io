ABOUT US

Insight Share

1 new friend request

We are a gamification-based reading community, serving anyone with the interest to read and gain insights. We work on a subscription basis, and books are read in any preferred version, preferably digital version, with EPUB format, using Adobe Digital Editions. We aim to gamify the entire process of 'insighting', a word we created to mean creating and sharing insights from reading.
